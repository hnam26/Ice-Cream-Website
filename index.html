Origins

Sweet Life ice cream shop was founded in 2015 by Maria Bianco, a former pastry chef who wanted to share her passion for frozen desserts with the community. She started with a small cart in the local park, offering homemade ice cream flavors made with fresh ingredients and natural sweeteners. Soon, her ice cream became popular among the locals and tourists alike, and she decided to open a brick-and-mortar shop in the downtown area. Ms Bianco's Sweet Life ice cream shop now serves a variety of flavors, toppings, and treats, all made with high-quality ingredients. Ms Bianco's motto is "Life is sweet, enjoy it with ice cream".
